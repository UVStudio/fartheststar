Rick Rose - Bio

I love editing, and I will make your editing team stronger – both creatively and technically.


Most recently, I had the privilege of locking 3 of the first 4 leicas on Season 5 as the lead editor of the hit children’s television show “Bubble Guppies” (Nick Jr.). I have also cut dozens of hilarious animatics for “Rusty Rivets” (Nick Jr.) and “Kody Kapow” (NBC/ Universal Kids).


On Season 2 of “Final Space” (TBS), my editing role was a technical one - I developed best practices with the team to handle the complicated workflow of that show at Jam Filled.


My experience doesn’t stop with animation: I have cut behind the scenes mini-docs for “When Calls the Heart” (Hallmark) as well as two other feature films. I have edited over 60 scripted videos including commercials, promos, industrials, and testimonials. I have had the pleasure of editing live performance music videos of Cypress Hill, Charles Bradley, As I Lay Dying, as well as highlight videos for two prominent Canadian Music Festivals. As well, I have edited and directed a number of short fiction pieces, receiving festival play and awards in the process.


Lastly, I’m a drummer with great timing – this skill has been invaluable in the edit suite.


I look forward to introducing myself in person. Connect with me here on LinkedIn.
